About Me

Chelsea Monahan is a data analyst and project implementation leader working at the intersection of education, data science and institutional relations. As the Director of Operations for the Denver Language School, Monahan led the opening of a second charter school campus to increase the delivery of K-8 language immersion curricula by 45%. She worked as the Managing Director for an edu-tainment start-up, producing an app that reached #2 in Appleâ€™s iTunes store in 2012, and placed app-based learning products in more than 14,000 major retailers worldwide. Monahan holds a B.A. in Political Economy from the University of California, Berkeley and a M.A. in International Relations from University of California, San Diego.
